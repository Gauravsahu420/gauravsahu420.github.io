Lily

😊The lily is a symbol of purity and refined beauty. The name Lily can also be short for Elizabeth, Lillian, Liliana or Lilith.😍

Holly

😉Holly is a symbol of goodwill and a much-loved traditional Christmas decoration. The name Holly is often given to girls born in winter.😇

Jasmine

😎The jasmine flower is a symbol of love, modesty and grace. They're often worn by women in their hair in southern and southeast Asia..😃

Daisy

😊The daisy is a symbol of gentleness, innocence, and loyal love. Daisy means "day's eye". This refers to the way the flower opens and closes with the sun.😆

Poppy

🙂The name Poppy is closely associated with calm and peace. It's sometimes used as a shortened version of Penelope.😳

Rose

😍Rose is a classic English girls' name, derived from the Latin rosa. Traditionally, while red roses are a symbol of love, romance and desire.😘

Alyssum

😎The name Alyssa is thought to have been inspired by the alyssum flower. Alyssum symbolises worth beyond beauty.😏

Iris

🌞The iris is a symbol of faith and wisdom. In Greek mythology, Iris was goddess of the rainbow. These days, it's a classic vintage name.🌝

Violet

🌱The violet is a symbol of modesty and simplicity. Viola and Violete are less common variations of the name Violet. 🌹

Ivy

🌻In ancient Greece, an Ivy wreath would be presented to newlyweds as a symbol of fertility.🌷
